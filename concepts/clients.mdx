Before VERA can analyze campaigns, score leads, or generate reports for a business, you need to create a client record for them. It takes less than a minute.

## Creating a client

Go to **Clients** in the sidebar and click **Add client**. You can also import clients directly from GoHighLevel — VERA creates a record for each location you select.

The key fields to fill in:

| Field | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Name** | How the client appears throughout VERA |
| **Monthly revenue** | Used to track billing health and flag potential churn |
| **Website** | Required to run SEO audits |
| **Industry** | Helps VERA benchmark performance against similar agencies |
| **Meta ad account ID** | Connects this client's Meta campaigns |
| **GoHighLevel location ID** | Links their CRM contacts and pipeline |
| **Google Ads customer ID** | Connects their Google campaigns |

You don't need to fill in every field on day one. Start with the name and connect platform accounts as you go.

## Client portal

You can generate a shareable link that lets your client view their campaign overview, pipeline, and reports — without logging in to VERA.

Go to the client's profile page and click **Generate portal link**. The link is read-only. Your client can see their data but can't make any changes.

<Note>
  No VERA account is needed on your client's end — the portal link works for anyone you share it with.
</Note>
