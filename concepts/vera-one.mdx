Vera One is what makes VERA more than a dashboard. It runs continuously in the background, watching your connected platforms, and surfaces the things that matter — without you having to dig for them.

## What Vera One does for you

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Alerts" icon="bell">
    Flags campaign issues automatically — CPL spikes, budget problems, underperforming ad sets — so you catch problems before they cost you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lead scoring" icon="star">
    Scores every lead 0–100 based on how likely they are to convert, updated every six hours automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance benchmarks" icon="chart-line">
    Compares your campaign results against similar agencies so you know if your numbers are actually good.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Churn risk" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Flags clients who may be at risk based on campaign activity and engagement trends.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Goal tracking" icon="bullseye">
    Checks whether your active campaigns match your agency's stated goals and alerts you when they drift.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Weekly summaries" icon="calendar">
    Auto-generates a performance summary for each client every week — no manual work required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Where to find Vera One insights

You don't need to go looking for insights — Vera One surfaces them where you already work.

| Where | What you'll see |
|---|---|
| **Intelligence** page | All alerts, signals, and AI-generated findings in one feed |
| **Dashboard** | Top alerts and campaign health at a glance |
| **Pipeline** page | Your top leads ranked by score with recommended next actions |
| **Clients** page | Churn risk indicator per client |
| **Reports** page | Attribution and goal alignment summary |

The **Intelligence** page is the best place to start your day.

## How it gets smarter over time

Vera One uses anonymized, aggregated performance data from across all VERA workspaces to build benchmarks for your industry. The more data it has, the more accurate your CPL targets, lead scores, and recommendations become.

<Note>
  Your individual data is never shared with or exposed to other agencies. Benchmarks are aggregated and anonymized across the network.
</Note>
