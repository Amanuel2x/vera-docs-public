When you sign up, VERA creates one workspace for your agency. Think of it as your agency's account: all your clients, connected platforms, and settings live here.

## What's inside your workspace

  <Card title="Clients" icon="building">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Your connected platforms — Meta Ads, GoHighLevel, Google Ads, Fathom, Calendly, and Stripe.

  <Card title="Campaigns" icon="bullhorn">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Intelligence" icon="brain">

## Workspace settings

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar to update your workspace.

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Workspace name** | Your agency's display name inside VERA |
| **User type** | Whether you're an agency, freelancer, or brand — used to personalize recommendations |
| **Branding** | Upload your logo and set an accent color for client-facing reports |

## Client limits

Your plan determines how many clients you can add:

- **Solo** — 1 client
- **Agency** — up to your plan's client seat limit
- **Business** — unlimited

You can check your current usage under **Settings → Billing**.
