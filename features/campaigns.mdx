The Campaigns page shows live performance across all your clients' Meta and Google Ads campaigns in one place. No switching between ad accounts.

## Viewing campaigns

Filter by platform (Meta or Google), status (active, paused), or client to narrow the list. Each campaign shows:

| Metric | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Spend** | Total ad spend for the selected date range |
| **Impressions** | How many times the ad was shown |
| **Clicks** | Total link clicks |
| **CPL** | Cost per lead |
| **CTR** | Click-through rate |
| **ROAS** | Return on ad spend |

The default date range is the last 30 days. You can switch to 7, 14, or 90 days.

## Pausing and resuming campaigns

Click **Pause** or **Resume** on any campaign row. The change takes effect on the platform immediately.

<Warning>
  Pausing a campaign stops all ad delivery right away. Double-check before pausing active campaigns.
</Warning>

## Build a campaign with VERA

**Campaigns → Build with VERA** walks you through launching a new campaign with AI-assisted recommendations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in the basics">
    Select the platform (Meta or Google), client, campaign objective, daily budget, and target audience. You can leave fields blank — VERA fills them in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get recommendations">
    Click **Recommend**. VERA suggests targeting, optimization goal, and a CPL target based on your historical data and industry benchmarks. It explains its reasoning in plain language for every suggestion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review any warnings">
    VERA flags potential issues before you spend anything — like a missing pixel, no landing page, or a budget below the recommended minimum. Review and address these before launching.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Click **Launch**. VERA creates the campaign, ad set, and ad in one step. Everything launches paused — activate it in Meta Ads Manager when you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automatic campaign monitoring

VERA checks all active campaigns every 4 hours and alerts you when something needs attention:

- **CPL spike** — cost per lead has risen significantly above your benchmark
- **Budget pacing** — spend is tracking to over- or under-deliver by month end
- **Underperformer** — a campaign has been below baseline with no recent changes

Alerts appear in your Intelligence feed. You can also set up the Action Engine to automatically pause or notify you when a CPL spike is detected.
