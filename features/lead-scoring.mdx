Every lead in your workspace gets scored automatically on a 0–100 scale. Scores update every 6 hours, so your list stays current without any manual effort.

## What the score is based on

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Fit">
    How well the lead matches your ideal customer — their industry, location, and profile compared to your historical wins.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Engagement">
    The depth and recency of their interaction history — messages, replies, meetings booked, and calls completed. A lead who replied yesterday ranks higher than one who's been quiet for weeks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Timing">
    How fast they're moving through your pipeline. A lead who went from inquiry to booked call in 48 hours scores higher than one sitting untouched for a month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Intent">
    Behavioral signals — form submissions, ad clicks, landing page conversions, and pipeline stage changes that indicate they're ready to buy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Where to see lead scores

**Pipeline page** — your top 10 leads are shown with their scores and a recommended next action for each one.

**Intelligence feed** — when a lead's score changes significantly, VERA surfaces it here.

**Client detail page** — view all leads for a specific client with their scores and history.

## Automatic actions based on scores

You can set up the Action Engine to respond to lead scores automatically. The default ruleset includes:

- When a lead scores **80 or higher** — VERA creates a follow-up task in GoHighLevel so nothing falls through the cracks
- When a lead scores **70 or higher** — VERA fires a qualified lead conversion event back to your connected ad platforms to improve attribution

You can adjust these thresholds or add your own rules in **Automations**.

<Tip>
  The Pipeline page is the fastest way to see your highest-priority leads each day — scores, reasoning, and recommended actions are all in one place.
</Tip>
