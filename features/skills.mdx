---

Skills are VERA's on-demand AI analysis tools. Pick a skill, select a client, click **Run** — VERA pulls the relevant live data and returns a structured analysis in 10–30 seconds.

## How to run a skill

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a client">
    Go to **Clients** and click on any client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Skills tab">
    Click the **Skills** tab on the client detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a skill and run it">
    Select a skill from the dropdown and click **Run**. Results appear within 10–30 seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

All past skill runs are saved. You can scroll back through previous outputs at any time to track how things have changed.

## Available skills

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Meta Ads Analysis">
    Reviews your client's live Meta campaign data and returns a performance summary — health score, CPL trends, what's working, what isn't, and recommended next actions.

    **Requires:** Meta Ads connected
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SEO Audit">
    Audits the client's website and returns an overall SEO score, a list of critical issues, and a prioritized quick-wins list.

    **Requires:** Website URL set on the client profile
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lead Scorer">
    Scores your client's leads 0–100 across fit, engagement, timing, and intent. Returns the score, a breakdown by dimension, and a recommended action for each lead.

    Runs automatically every 6 hours — you can also trigger it on demand.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign Monitor">
    Checks live campaign performance against benchmarks and flags underperformers, budget issues, and predicted month-end outcomes.

    Runs automatically every 4 hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Meeting Analysis">
    Analyzes a Fathom sales call recording and returns a summary, action items, buying signals, and recommended next steps.

    **Requires:** Fathom connected
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Content Writing">
    Generates ad copy, social posts, emails, blog posts, and offer copy tailored to the client's industry and goals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign Builder">
    Builds a recommended campaign structure — objective, targeting, budget, and expected CPL — based on your historical data and industry benchmarks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Weekly Summary">
    Generates a concise weekly performance summary for a client — key wins, concerns, and action items. Auto-generates every Friday.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monthly Report">
    Generates a complete monthly report with an executive summary, campaign section, SEO section, and next-month plan. Auto-generates on the 1st of each month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Outbound Sequence Builder">
    Designs a complete multi-step outbound email sequence — step strategy, timing, and expected results. Run this first, then use Outbound Email Writer to write each step.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Outbound Email Writer">
    Writes a personalized cold email for a specific step in your outbound sequence, keeping messaging consistent across the full sequence.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Skill history

Every run is saved automatically. Return to the **Skills** tab on any client to see all past outputs.
