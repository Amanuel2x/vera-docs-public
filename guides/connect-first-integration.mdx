---
title: "Connect your first integration"
description: "How to link Meta Ads or GoHighLevel to VERA and get your data syncing."
---

Connecting a platform is the first thing you should do after creating your workspace. Once an integration is live, VERA pulls in your historical data and starts surfacing insights automatically.

## Which platform to connect first

Start with **Meta Ads** or **GoHighLevel** — they unlock the most features. If you only have one available right now, connect it and add the other later.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Setup">
    Click **Setup** in the left sidebar. You'll see a card for each supported platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click the **Connect** button on the platform you want to add. VERA redirects you to that platform's authorization screen.

    For GoHighLevel using a private integration token, you'll paste your token and Location ID directly into the card instead.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize and return">
    Complete the authorization and come back to VERA. The card updates to show a green connected state with your account name.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for the initial sync">
    VERA immediately starts pulling in your historical data — campaigns, contacts, pipeline stages, meetings — from that platform.

    <Note>
      The initial sync can take a few minutes for large accounts. A progress indicator shows on the Setup card while it runs. Your data starts appearing across VERA once the sync finishes.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your data">
    Once the sync is done:
    - Campaigns appear on the **Campaigns** page
    - Contacts and pipeline stages show up on the **Pipeline** page
    - Leads start getting scored in the **Intelligence** feed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get from each integration

**Meta Ads**
- All campaigns, ad sets, and performance data appear in VERA
- Leads from Meta lead forms start syncing and get scored automatically
- Conversion events are forwarded to improve attribution accuracy

**GoHighLevel**
- Contacts and pipeline stages sync across all your locations
- Stage changes (booked, showed, closed) feed back into your campaign attribution
- The Action Engine can create GHL tasks automatically when a hot lead is detected

<Tip>
  Connect both Meta Ads and GoHighLevel for the best results. When both are connected, VERA can trace the full path from an ad click to a closed deal — that's where the most useful insights come from.
</Tip>

## Disconnecting an integration

Go to **Setup**, click the connected platform's card, and select **Disconnect**. This stops future syncing. Your existing data in VERA is not deleted.
