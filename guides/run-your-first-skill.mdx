---
title: "Run your first AI skill"
description: "How to run an AI analysis on a client in VERA and read the results."
---

AI skills analyze your client's live data and return structured, actionable findings — no prompting required. This guide walks you through running one for the first time.

## Before you start

Make sure you have:
- At least one client added to your workspace
- At least one integration connected (Meta Ads for campaign analysis, or any source for lead scoring)

If you haven't connected an integration yet, see [Connect your first integration](/guides/connect-first-integration).

## Run a skill

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a client">
    Click **Clients** in the left sidebar, then click on the client you want to analyze.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the Skills tab">
    Click the **Skills** tab on the client detail page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a skill">
    Select a skill from the dropdown. Good starting points:

    - **Meta Ads Analysis** — reviews campaign performance and tells you what to fix. Requires Meta Ads connected.
    - **Lead Scorer** — scores your leads so you know who to contact first. Works with any connected data.
    - **SEO Audit** — audits the client's website. Requires a website URL on the client profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Run">
    Click **Run**. VERA pulls the latest data and runs the analysis. Results appear in 10–30 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the output">
    Scroll down to see the full AI output. Each skill returns structured findings and specific recommended actions — not just a summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

All skill runs are saved automatically. Come back to the Skills tab any time to see the history and track how things change over time.

<Tip>
  If a skill comes back with no data, check that the relevant integration is connected and the initial data sync has finished. You can check sync status on the **Setup** page.
</Tip>
