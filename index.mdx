## What VERA does for your agency

VERA brings all your marketing data into one place and acts on it automatically. Here's how the platform works end-to-end:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your platforms">
    Link Meta Ads, Google Ads, GoHighLevel CRM, Fathom meeting recordings, Calendly bookings, and Stripe payments from the **Setup** page. OAuth flows handle authentication — no manual token management required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let VERA backfill your data">
    After connecting, VERA automatically syncs historical campaigns, contacts, conversations, and meetings into the intelligence layer. This powers accurate benchmarks from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run AI skills on your clients">
    Choose from 11 built-in AI skills — campaign analysis, lead scoring, SEO audits, content writing, meeting analysis, outbound sequences, and more. Each skill runs against real client data and returns actionable output.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up automated actions">
    Create rules in the Action Engine to respond to signals automatically: pause underperforming campaigns, tag hot leads in your CRM, send Telegram alerts when CPL spikes, or enroll contacts in outbound sequences.
  </Step>
</Steps>
