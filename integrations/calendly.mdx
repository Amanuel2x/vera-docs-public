---
title: "Connect Calendly Bookings to VERA"
description: "Sync Calendly bookings into VERA and automatically forward confirmed booking events to Meta, TikTok, and Google Ads for accurate conversion attribution."
---

Connecting Calendly closes the loop between your ad spend and booked meetings. Every confirmed booking creates an intelligence event in Vera One and fires a conversion signal to all connected ad platforms — so Meta, TikTok, and Google Ads all learn from your booking outcomes.

## What connecting Calendly unlocks

- **Booking sync** — confirmed Calendly events appear in VERA with invitee details, event type, start time, and location
- **Conversion forwarding** — every `invitee.created` webhook fires a booking conversion event to all connected ad platforms simultaneously via VERA's funnel event bus
- **Lead cross-reference** — VERA cross-references booked invitee emails against your Meta leads, marking which leads converted to meetings
- **Prospect funnel visibility** — bookings feed the **Meeting Set** stage in the prospect funnel view (Prospects → Contacted → Meeting Set → Proposal)

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Setup">
    In the VERA dashboard, navigate to **Settings → Setup**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect on the Calendly card">
    VERA redirects you to Calendly's OAuth consent screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize VERA">
    Sign in to Calendly and grant access. VERA requests the following scopes: `event_types:read`, `scheduled_events:read`, `scheduled_events:write`, `users:read`, `organizations:read`, `webhooks:read`, `webhooks:write`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to VERA">
    After authorizing, Calendly redirects you back to VERA. A webhook subscription is registered automatically so VERA receives booking events in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The webhook subscription is registered automatically during the OAuth flow. VERA listens for `invitee.created`, `invitee.canceled`, and `invitee_no_show.created` events.
</Note>

## Token auto-refresh

VERA automatically refreshes your Calendly access token when it expires. On any `401 Unauthorized` response from the Calendly API, VERA uses the stored refresh token to obtain a new access token and updates the stored credentials — no reconnection required.

## How booking events reach your ad platforms

When Calendly sends a confirmed booking event to VERA:

1. VERA creates an `IntelligenceEvent` of type `booking` for the workspace.
2. VERA dispatches a `CompleteRegistration` conversion event to all connected ad platforms (Meta CAPI, TikTok Events API, Google Ads Conversions) via the funnel event bus.
3. VERA cross-references the invitee email against existing Meta leads and marks matched leads as `calendly_booked = true`.

All three steps happen within the same webhook handler — typically within a few seconds of the booking being confirmed.

<Tip>
  Connecting Calendly alongside Meta Ads means Meta's algorithm receives a `CompleteRegistration` server-side event for every booked meeting. This improves optimization for lead generation campaigns and typically reduces CPL over time.
</Tip>

## Syncing bookings in the background

In addition to real-time webhook events, VERA's background task scheduler syncs Calendly events every 4 hours to catch any events that may have been missed. Historical bookings from the past 90 days are available when you first connect.
