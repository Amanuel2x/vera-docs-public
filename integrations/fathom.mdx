---
title: "Connect Fathom Meeting Recordings to VERA"
description: "Sync Fathom meeting recordings into VERA and run AI analysis to extract call scores, sentiment, action items, objections, and buying signals."
---

Connecting Fathom brings your meeting recordings into VERA's intelligence layer. Once connected, VERA can list your recorded meetings, pull transcripts and summaries, and run the `meeting-analysis` skill to score calls and surface actionable insights.

## What connecting Fathom unlocks

- **Meeting sync** — all your Fathom recorded meetings are available in VERA, including titles, participants, dates, and durations
- **Transcript and summary access** — full transcripts and Fathom-generated summaries are pulled for each meeting
- **AI meeting analysis** — the `meeting-analysis` skill scores the call (0–100), classifies sentiment, extracts action items, identifies objections, and surfaces buying signals
- **Intelligence events** — every analyzed meeting logs a signal to the Vera One intelligence layer, feeding lead scoring and client health tracking

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Setup">
    In the VERA dashboard, navigate to **Settings → Setup**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect on the Fathom card">
    VERA redirects you to Fathom's OAuth consent screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize VERA">
    Sign in to Fathom and grant VERA access. VERA requests the `public_api` scope.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to VERA">
    After authorizing, Fathom redirects you back to the Setup page. VERA immediately begins a background sync of your recent meetings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Token auto-refresh

VERA automatically refreshes your Fathom access token when it expires. If a request to Fathom returns a `401 Unauthorized` response, VERA uses the stored refresh token to obtain a new access token and retries the request — without any action required from you.

<Note>
  Auto-refresh requires a valid refresh token stored from your original OAuth authorization. If you revoke VERA's access in Fathom, you will need to reconnect from the Setup page.
</Note>

## What data syncs

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Meeting title | Name of the recorded meeting |
| Participants / attendees | Names of people on the call |
| Meeting date and duration | When the meeting occurred and how long it ran |
| Transcript | Full meeting transcript |
| Summary | Fathom-generated summary of the call |

## How to analyze a meeting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Intelligence">
    In the VERA dashboard, navigate to **Intelligence**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a meeting">
    Find the meeting you want to analyze in the meetings list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Analyze">
    VERA runs the `meeting-analysis` skill on the transcript and summary. The skill uses Claude to produce a structured analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The analysis result includes:

| Output | Description |
|---|---|
| `call_score` | 0–100 score reflecting overall call quality |
| `sentiment` | `positive`, `neutral`, or `negative` |
| `transcript_summary` | AI-generated summary of what was discussed |
| `action_items` | Follow-up tasks identified from the conversation |
| `objections` | Objections raised by the prospect |
| `buying_signals` | Indicators of purchase intent detected in the conversation |

<Tip>
  You can associate a meeting analysis with a specific client by passing a `client_id` when triggering the analysis. This links the call score and signals to the client's intelligence profile.
</Tip>

## Syncing meetings in the background

VERA's background task scheduler automatically syncs new Fathom meetings every 2 hours. You do not need to manually trigger a sync after each meeting — new recordings appear in VERA automatically.
