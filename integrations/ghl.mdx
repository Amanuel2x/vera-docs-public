---
title: "Connect GoHighLevel CRM to VERA"
description: "Sync contacts, pipeline stages, and conversation history from GoHighLevel, and trigger CRM actions automatically from Vera One signals."
---

GoHighLevel is the primary CRM layer in VERA. Connecting it lets Vera One read your contacts and pipeline, attribute Meta leads to GHL contacts, and take automated actions — tagging contacts, creating tasks, and enrolling sequences — based on real-time intelligence signals.

## What connecting GoHighLevel unlocks

- **Contact sync** — pull all contacts from your GHL sub-account into VERA for lead scoring and attribution
- **Pipeline stage management** — view opportunities grouped by stage; stage changes from GHL are received by VERA automatically via webhook
- **Conversation history** — full message history per contact, available in the intelligence layer
- **Lead attribution** — Meta lead form submissions are matched to GHL contacts by email and phone
- **CRM actions via Action Engine** — tag contacts, create tasks, and enroll sequences triggered by Vera One signals (e.g. a high lead score, a stage change, a payment event)

## How to connect

VERA uses GoHighLevel's OAuth v2 flow. The connection involves two steps: authorizing at the agency (company) level to discover your sub-accounts, then authorizing at the sub-account (location) level to operate inside it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Setup">
    In the VERA dashboard, navigate to **Settings → Setup**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect on the GoHighLevel card">
    VERA redirects you to the GHL OAuth consent screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize and pick your sub-account">
    Log in to GoHighLevel and select the sub-account (location) you want VERA to operate in. This is typically the account you manage campaigns and contacts from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to VERA">
    After authorizing, GHL redirects you back to VERA. A full backfill of your contacts, pipeline, and conversations begins automatically in the background.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The backfill runs after connect and does not block the redirect. Depending on your contact volume, it may take a few minutes to complete. Check the **Intelligence** page for backfill progress events.
</Note>

## What VERA backfills on connect

When you complete the GHL OAuth flow, VERA queues a full four-step backfill sequence:

1. **Meta Ads data** — existing campaign data from your connected Meta account
2. **Meta → GHL lead matching** — cross-references Meta leads against GHL contacts
3. **GHL contacts and pipeline** — full contact list and all open opportunities
4. **GHL conversations** — full message history per contact, paginated

## Lead attribution

When Meta Ads and GoHighLevel are both connected, VERA automatically matches each incoming Meta lead form submission to a GHL contact:

1. VERA searches GHL contacts by the lead's **email address**.
2. If no email match is found, VERA searches by **phone number**.
3. Matched leads are linked to their GHL contact ID. Unmatched leads remain in VERA and are re-evaluated on the next sync.

The funnel view in VERA uses this attribution to show the full prospect journey: **Prospects (Meta leads) → Contacted (matched GHL) → Meeting Set (Calendly booked) → Proposal**.

## CRM actions via Action Engine

Once GHL is connected, the Action Engine can trigger the following CRM actions in response to Vera One signals:

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| `tag_contact` | Adds one or more tags to a GHL contact. VERA merges new tags with existing ones — existing tags are never removed. |
| `create_ghl_task` | Creates a follow-up task in GHL for the contact associated with the signal. |
| `enroll_sequence` | Enrolls a contact in a GHL automation sequence. |

## Webhook events from GHL

After connecting, GHL sends pipeline stage change events to VERA automatically. These stage changes are used to:

- Update the funnel view in real time
- Dispatch conversion events to connected ad platforms (Meta CAPI, TikTok, Google Ads) when a contact moves to a booked, showed, or closed stage
- Trigger Action Engine rules that respond to `ghl_stage_change` signals

<Warning>
  If you reconnect GHL or change your sub-account, existing webhook registrations may need to be re-registered. Contact support if stage change events stop appearing in VERA after a reconnect.
</Warning>
