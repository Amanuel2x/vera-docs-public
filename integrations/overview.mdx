VERA connects to the platforms your agency already uses. Each integration you add gives Vera One more signal to work with — sharper lead scoring, more accurate attribution, and better campaign intelligence across your entire client roster.

You manage all integrations from **Settings → Setup** in the dashboard.

## Why connecting platforms matters

Every integration you add closes a gap in the attribution chain. When Meta Ads, GoHighLevel, Calendly, and Stripe are all connected, Vera One can trace the full journey from ad impression to closed payment — and use that data to score leads, benchmark campaigns against your network, and trigger automated actions at the right moment.

<Info>
  You do not need every integration connected to use VERA. Connect the platforms that are live for your agency first, then add others as you onboard clients.
</Info>
