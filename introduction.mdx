VERA is an AI-powered Agency Operating System built for marketing agencies, freelancers, and in-house brand teams. It connects your ad platforms, CRM, meeting tools, scheduling software, and payments into a single workspace — then uses Vera One, the built-in AI intelligence layer, to surface insights, score leads, and trigger automated actions based on real-time signals from across your stack.

## What VERA does

VERA brings your marketing data into one place and acts on it automatically. The platform is organized around five core capabilities:

**Multi-platform ad management** — Build, launch, monitor, and pause campaigns across Meta Ads and Google Ads from a unified dashboard. Campaign health is tracked continuously by background workers, so you are always looking at current performance data.

**Lead scoring** — Every lead is scored 0–100 across four dimensions: fit, engagement, timing, and intent. Scores update automatically on a regular schedule and surface in the Intelligence feed so you can prioritize outreach without manual review.

**AI skills** — VERA ships with a set of Claude-powered skill analyses you can run against any client: campaign performance breakdowns, meeting summaries, SEO audits, content generation, outbound sequence drafting, and more. Skills run against live client data and return structured, actionable output.

**Action automation** — The Action Engine lets you create event-driven rules that fire automatically: pause a campaign when CPL exceeds a threshold, tag a hot lead in your CRM, send an alert when churn risk rises, or enroll a contact in an outbound sequence. Rules have configurable cooldowns and can require confirmation before executing.

**Vera One intelligence layer** — Vera One is the decision layer that sits across everything. It aggregates signals from every connected platform, attributes revenue across the funnel, identifies at-risk clients, and drives the alerts and recommendations that appear in the Intelligence feed.

## Who VERA is for

VERA is designed for three types of users:

- **Marketing agencies** managing multiple client accounts across ad platforms and CRMs
- **Freelancers** running campaigns and reporting for individual clients
- **Brands** with in-house marketing teams who need a unified view of ad performance and lead flow

## Integrations at a glance

VERA connects to the tools your agency already uses:

| Integration | What VERA does with it |
|---|---|
| **Meta Ads** | Sync campaigns, pull insights, build ads, forward CAPI events |
| **GoHighLevel** | Read contacts, pipelines, and conversations; create and tag contacts |
| **Google Ads** | Read campaigns and performance data via the MCC manager account |
| **Fathom** | Sync meeting recordings and summaries for AI analysis |
| **Calendly** | Import booked events and map them to leads |
| **Stripe** | Track MRR, payment history, and subscription status per client |
