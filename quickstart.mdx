---
title: "Get started with VERA"
description: "Set up your workspace, connect your first integration, add a client, and run your first AI analysis — in about 15 minutes."
---

Follow these steps in order. Each one builds on the last.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.verahq.ai](https://app.verahq.ai) and sign up with your email.

    VERA creates a workspace for your agency automatically. You can rename it anytime from **Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect an integration">
    Go to **Setup** in the left sidebar and connect at least one platform. We recommend starting with **Meta Ads** or **GoHighLevel** — they give VERA the most to work with right away.

    Click **Connect** next to the platform you want and follow the authorization steps. VERA starts pulling in your historical data in the background right away.

    <Tip>
      You don't need to connect everything on day one. Start with whatever platform is most active for your agency.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first client">
    Go to **Clients** and click **Add client**. Fill in the client's name and monthly revenue. You can also import clients directly from GoHighLevel.

    Once you add a client, VERA uses it to organize everything — campaigns, leads, reports, and AI analysis are all tied to that client record.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run an AI skill">
    Open a client and click the **Skills** tab. Pick a skill to run:

    - **Meta Ads Analysis** — reviews campaign performance and flags what to fix
    - **Lead Scorer** — scores your leads so you know who to contact first
    - **SEO Audit** — audits the client's website and returns a prioritized issue list

    Click **Run**. Results appear within 10–30 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check the Intelligence feed">
    Go to **Intelligence** to see everything VERA has surfaced — campaign alerts, lead score changes, skill results, and recommendations.

    This is your daily command center. Check it each morning to see what needs your attention.
  </Step>
</Steps>
